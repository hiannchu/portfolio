#Branding #Logo

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Cïleo Café is more than just a coffee shop—it’s a heartwarming escape where coffee lovers and dog enthusiasts can come together. Inspired by the owner’s beloved golden retriever, Cïleo, the café creates a cozy, welcoming space where every sip feels like a little "pawfect" moment. Whether customers come for high-quality coffee, the comforting presence of dogs, or a peaceful break from the day, Cïleo Café promises an experience filled with warmth, companionship, and a touch of dos charm.

Brand Mission

1. To create a welcoming space where dog lovers and coffee enthusiasts can connect, unwind, and share joyful moments.
2. To become the go-to destination for dog-friendly coffee experiences, fostering a strong community of pet lovers and café-goers alike.

Brand Values

  • 1. Companionship – Coffee and dogs bring people together.
  • 2. Quality – High-standard coffee and customer experience.
  • 3. Warmth & Comfort – A space that feels like home.
  • 4. Inclusivity – Welcoming everyone, with or without pets.
  • 5. Community Engagement – Supporting local rescues, adoption programs, and dog-friendly initiatives.

Target Audience

  • 1. Dog Lovers & Pet Owners.
  • 2. Coffee Enthusiasts & Café Goers.
  • 3. Urban Professionals & Young Adults (20s-40s).
  • 4. Families & Weekend Explorers.
